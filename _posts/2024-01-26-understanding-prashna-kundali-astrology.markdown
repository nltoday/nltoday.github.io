### Aligning Life Events
To determine a person's birth time without records, astrologers analyze patterns in their major life events. ### Tapping Intuition in Prashna 
When no birth data exists, prashna kundali generates a horoscope from the query time instead. **However, its accuracy relies on the astrologer's sensitivity.** Since results depend on ambient conditions and the questioner's state of mind, **subtle nonverbal cues** must be interpreted. Sound, body language, and atmosphere all provide hints. **This requires highly developed intuitive faculties** to blend logic with less tangible factors.
### Astrological Calculations
Of course, simply feeling out a situation isn't enough. **Skilled prashna practitioners also apply technical analysis.** They’ll identify the sun and moon signs as a starting point. Then meticulous **mathematical derivations** aid the process of elimination to pin down likely birth timings. **Blending astrological computations with intuitive arts** characterizes the complete prashna approach.
### Developing the Human Instrument 
To truly excel at prashna, one must refine oneself as the diagnostic tool. **Years of ethical practice, meditation and character cultivation** are needed. Only by becoming attuned, centered and impartial can subtle energies be accurately perceived. **An enlightened inner state allows exterior signs to shine through clearly.** Unless the astrologerdevelops superior awareness and integrity, the method remains limited.
